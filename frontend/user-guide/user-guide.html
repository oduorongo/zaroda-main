🚀 Setup

School Onboarding

Get your school live on ZARODA SMS in minutes. Start with one class — the whole school can join later.

HOIAdmin

📝 Getting Started

1
Create your account
Sign up at app.zarodasolutions.app or use a teacher's invite link. Enter your school name, county, sub-county and zone.
2
Set up your first stream
Add one class (e.g. "Grade 4 North") to start. You can add more streams anytime. 2-week free trial, no card needed.
3
Register learners
Bulk upload via CSV or PDF, or add individually. The AI parser reads most PDF admission lists automatically.
4
Invite your teachers
Go to Staff → Invite. Each teacher receives a link to create their account and is assigned their stream(s).
5
Generate the timetable
ZARODA auto-generates a KICD-compliant timetable for each grade band. Review, adjust if needed, then publish.
6
Set up fees
Create your fee structure for the term. Generate invoices for the whole stream in one click.
⚠️ Your 2-week free trial covers all modules with no restrictions. After the trial, billing is KES 2,400/stream/year (primary/junior) or KES 3,360/stream/year (senior). Schools with 3+ streams get a 30% discount.
📚 Module

Academic Core & Timetable

Manage learners, streams, CBC assessments, report cards, and KICD-compliant timetables across all grade bands.

HOITeachersParentsLearners

🗓 KICD Timetable

The timetable engine follows the official Kenya MoE CBE Timetabling Guidelines exactly — 7 grade bands, correct lesson durations, break positions, and creative arts before breaks.

Pre-Primary: 25 lessons/week · 30 min Grade 1–3: 31 lessons/week · 30 min Grade 4–6: 35 lessons/week · 35 min Grade 7–9: 41 lessons/week · 40 min

📊 CBC Report Cards

ZARODA generates fully formatted CBC report cards aligned to KICD performance levels. AI writes personalised comments for each learner automatically.

💡 Performance levels: EE (Exceeding), ME (Meeting), AE (Approaching), BE (Below) for Grade 1–6. Grade 7–12 use EE1/EE2 through BE1/BE2.

👨‍🎓 Learner Registration

1
Go to Learners → Add Learner
Add individually or bulk upload. For bulk upload, upload the PDF admission list — AI extracts names, admission numbers, and DOBs.
2
Assign to a stream
Each learner belongs to one stream per academic year. Stream determines their timetable, teacher, and report card.
3
Link a parent account
Enter the guardian's phone number and email. They receive an SMS with login details to access the parent portal.
💰 Module

Finance & Fees

Full school financial management — fee collection, M-Pesa STK push, government fund tracking, payroll, and QASO-ready financial reports.

HOIBursarParents

📱 M-Pesa Payments

1
Parent pays via M-Pesa
Bursar enters parent's phone number and clicks "Send STK Push". Parent receives a prompt on their phone to enter their PIN.
2
Automatic reconciliation
Once parent approves, ZARODA automatically matches the payment to the learner's account using their admission number. No manual entry needed.
3
Receipt generated instantly
A receipt (RCP-YYYY-NNNNN) is created and sent to the parent. The cashbook is updated automatically.

🏛 Government Funds

Track FPE, FDJSE, and FDSSE receipts and expenditure separately, with vote head enforcement per the Kenya Handbook on Financial Management.

ℹ️ ZARODA enforces the correct vote heads per fund type. For example, FPE expenditure cannot be posted under "Assessment & Examinations" — that vote head is only valid for FDJSE and FDSSE.
📢 Module

Communication

SMS, email, push notifications, WhatsApp — reach parents, teachers, and learners with the right message at the right time.

HOITeachersParents

📣 Announcements

Post school announcements to targeted audiences: All, Admins only, Teachers only, Learners only, or Parents only.

💡 Set priority to Urgent for emergency announcements — they appear at the top of every user's feed with a red highlight and trigger an immediate push notification.

💸 Fee Reminders

Send bulk fee reminders to all parents with outstanding balances in one click. ZARODA automatically personalises each message with the learner's name, balance, and due date. Choose SMS, WhatsApp, or email.

💬 Parent–Teacher Messages

1
Parent starts a conversation
From the parent portal, tap Messages → New Message. Select the learner — the class teacher is automatically linked.
2
Teacher receives notification
A push notification alerts the teacher. They reply from their dashboard. Messages are threaded by learner.
📋 Module

Professional Records

Full KICD CBC document pipeline powered by AI. Generate a complete scheme of work, lesson plans, lesson notes, and learner progress records in minutes.

TeachersHOI

🤖 AI Document Pipeline

1
Generate Scheme of Work
Select subject, grade, and term. AI reads the KICD syllabus and generates all strands, sub-strands, SLOs, Key Inquiry Questions, and activities for every week.
2
Generate Lesson Plans
For each week in your scheme, generate a full CBC lesson plan: Introduction → Development → Conclusion, plus Core Competencies, Values, and PCIs.
3
Generate Lesson Notes
From each lesson plan, generate detailed teaching notes: board work, worked examples, probing questions, and expected learner responses.
4
Submit for HOI Approval
Submit your documents from the Teacher Folder. HOI reviews and approves, requests revision, or rejects with comments.
⚠️ Suspension and expulsion actions require HOI approval before taking effect. The system blocks these actions until an HOI or Deputy reviews and approves.
📖 Module · FREE

Library

Full library management — book catalogue, borrowing, returns, inventory. Completely free. No fines, no charges of any kind.

TeachersHOILearners
✅ The library module is 100% free — included in every ZARODA SMS subscription at no extra cost. No overdue fines, no charges, no penalties of any kind.

📦 Issue a Book

1
Scan the barcode
Scan the book's barcode using a USB scanner or type the accession number (e.g. LIB-2025-0001). Book details appear instantly.
2
Enter borrower
Search for the learner or teacher by name or admission number. Select borrower type and confirm.
3
Confirm issue
Click "Issue Book". Due date is set automatically (14 days by default). No fines if returned late — only a gentle reminder.

⏰ Late Returns

If a book is past its due date, the Library → Late Returns tab shows a reminder list. Use the "Send Reminder" button to notify the borrower. No fines are charged.

🏆 Module

Sports Management

School-level sports — teams, inter-class competitions, athlete profiles, AI talent analytics. Push qualified teams to ZARODA Sports Base for cross-school championships.

HOIGames DeptTeachers

🏟 School-Level Sports

Manage internal competitions (inter-class, inter-house), team rosters, fixtures, and results. All within your school — no external connectivity needed.

🚀 Qualifying for Championships

1
Create a Qualification Register
Go to Sports → Qualifications → New. Select the discipline, competition level (Zone/County/National), and how the team qualified.
2
Add qualified athletes
Add athletes to the register with their events and personal bests. For team sports, select squad members.
3
Push to ZARODA Sports Base
Browse open Base championships, click "Register School for this Championship". Bib numbers are assigned automatically. Free.

⭐ AI Talent Analytics

ZARODA AI analyses each athlete's recent performances and generates a talent score (0–10), strengths, areas to improve, and a personalised recommendation for the coach.

⚖️ Module

Discipline & Guidance

Record incidents, track discipline actions, manage counselling sessions, and generate QASO-ready behaviour reports.

HOITeachersCounsellors

📋 Recording an Incident

1
Go to Discipline → Record Incident
Search for the learner, select the severity (minor / moderate / major / critical), and write a factual description.
2
Log the action taken
Record what action was taken (verbal warning, detention, parent meeting, etc.). Suspensions and expulsions require HOI approval.
3
Notify the parent
Use the "Notify Parent" button to log the communication. Choose the channel (call, SMS, WhatsApp, email, in-person).
🔒 Counselling records are confidential. Only the counsellor who recorded the session and the HOI can view session notes. All other users see only that a session took place — not the content.

📈 QASO Report

Generate a ready-to-submit QASO report from Analytics → QASO Report. The report covers the full term: incident counts by severity, suspensions, counselling sessions, and repeat offenders — formatted for the Sub-County Quality Assurance and Standards Officer.

Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to the most common questions about ZARODA SMS.

💰 Billing

KES 2,400 per stream per year for primary and junior schools (Grade 1–9). KES 3,360 per stream per year for senior schools (Grade 10–12). Schools with 3 or more streams get a 30% discount. There is a 2-week free trial — no credit card required to start.

🔧 Technical

ZARODA is a web-based progressive web app (PWA). You need internet to load data and save changes. However, recently viewed pages are cached and can be read offline. The mobile app (coming soon) will have offline data entry that syncs when reconnected.
Yes. ZARODA SMS is fully mobile-responsive and works on any smartphone browser. All core features — fee collection, attendance, announcements — are optimised for small screens. Install it as a PWA from your browser for a native app feel.
ZARODA integrates with Safaricom's Daraja API (M-Pesa for Business). You need an M-Pesa for Business (Paybill) account. Your bursar enters the API credentials in Finance → Settings. STK push, C2B, and auto-reconciliation all work once configured.

📚 CBC / Academic

Yes. ZARODA is built from the ground up for Kenya's CBC/CBE framework. The timetable engine uses the official MoE CBE Timetabling Guidelines. Professional records follow KICD document formats. Report cards use CBC performance levels. The AI uses the KICD syllabus as the reference for all generated documents.
