# CBC Learning Areas per Grade — from official KICD Assessment Report Books

These are the learning areas read from the **section/table titles** of the uploaded KICD
grade assessment report books (Playgroup → Grade 9). Summative-assessment tables and
comment/signature sections were intentionally excluded. This is the authoritative set the
assessment rubric (and therefore the mark list and report cards) should reflect.

The first term in each book carries the COMPLETE set; some books drop areas (notably English)
in Terms 2–3 — that omission is what caused "Grade 8 English missing". The complete set is used.

## Pre-Primary
- **Playgroup**: Mathematics Activities, Language Activities, Creative Activities,
  Environmental Activities, Religious Education Activities (CRE)
- **PP1 / PP2**: Mathematics Activities, English Language Activities, Environmental Activities,
  Creative Arts Activities, Christian Religious Education Activities

## Lower Primary (Activities naming)
- **Grade 1 / 2 / 3**: English Language Activities, Shughuli Za Kiswahili (Kiswahili),
  Indeginous Language Activities, Mathematics Activities, Environmental Activities,
  Creative Arts Activities, Christian Religious Education Activities

## Upper Primary (Activities naming)
- **Grade 4 / 5 / 6**: English Language Activities, Shughuli Za Kiswahili,
  Indeginous Language Activities, Mathematics Activities, Science & Technology Activities,
  Agriculture & Nutrition Activities, Social Studies Activities, Creative Arts Activities,
  C.R.E Activities

## Junior School (no "Activities" suffix)
- **Grade 7 / 8 / 9**: English Language, Kiswahili, Mathematics, Integrated Science
  (spelled "Intergrated Science" in the books), Social Studies, Agriculture & Nutrition,
  Pre-Technical Studies (spelled "Pretechnical Studies"), Creative Arts, C.R.E

## Note on naming
The seed deliberately keeps each grade's existing spelling (e.g. "Mathematics Activities",
"C.R.E", "Intergrated Science", "Shughuli Za Kiswahili") so the mark-list and report-card
matching — which compares learning-area names — continues to line up. If the school later
standardises spellings (e.g. "Integrated Science"), update the templates and the mark-list
display together.
